Why?

Biotech needs more transparency and knowledge sharing.

This newsletter is for those interested in the inner workings, opinions, and break-room discussions within biotech. Whether you're a student, researcher, industry-guru, or from another industry, BioBreaks provides access to leaders' candid opinions. BioBreaks contributors are listed at the end of each email, while their individual quotes are kept anonymous. Inspired by the Tech Caucus.

Who?

Founders

Starting up and scaling into a sustainable business is thrilling, and hard. They'll tell you why.

Investors

In tune with the trends, they've seen it all (mostly).

Journalists

Always reporting on what's breaking in biotech, gives them a well-thought-out perspective.

Researchers

Working in the trenches to develop new technologies, they critique the core science of new applications.
